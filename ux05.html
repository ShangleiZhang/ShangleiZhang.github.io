Retail Interactive System

  • April,2016
  • Application Conceptual Design

Project Info

This is a two-day design challenge that requires participants to brainstorm a digital and interactive system to help people explore the amenities and services in a mega scale shopping center. The primary target users are those shoppers who have never visited the shopping center before.

* This is an individual project.

What I Did

  • UX Research
  • Rapid Prototyping
  • UX/UI Conceptual Design

Project Description

This two-day project is to create a digital, interactive system to help people discover amenities and services at a mega shopping mall/center. The primary target audien are those people who have never visited the shopping mall/center before. The system should include at least a map.

Since the target users is clearly defined, I started from understanding what is shopping activitied and the shopping process, which is also called shopping journey.

Form

Since the device will be displayed in the public area of a shopping mall, the product appearance design is crucial. Related to the "Modular Man" created by architect Le Corbusier, the size of the device should be suitable for people standing at different distances.
