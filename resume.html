Shanglei's Story

Shanglei is a multi-disciplinary designer, working in human-computer interaction, architecture, and graphic design. She began her career as an Architectural Designer in Singapore in 2010. Five years later, Shanglei moved to Indianapolis to pursue a master's degree.

After graduating from SOIC, Indiana University-Purdue University with a Master degree in Human-Computer Interaction in May 2017, Shanglei joined Inverse-Square as UI/UX Specialist.

Her hybrid background gives Shanglei a unique skill set to bring to her current role; she is able to take a project from ambiguity to clarity by setting a direction that combines client, business, and development goals.

In her spare time, Shanglei is a traveler, a nature enthusiast, and a coffee addict. She has already traveled to a dozen countries, and looks forward to exploring more, meeting diverse people, and savoring the moments with a good cup of coffee.
