Shanglei's Story

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website!

I am Shanglei, an Architect turned UX/UI Designer. I began my career as an Architectural/graphic Designer in Singapore from 2010. Five years later, I moved to Indianapolis, IN to pursue a master's degree in Human-Computer Interaction and reunited with my family.

After graduating from SOIC, Indiana University-Purdue University in May 2017, I joined Inverse-Square as a UI/UX Specialist.

I appreciate the value of my multidisciplinary background, which gives me a unique skill set to bring myself to my current role; With years of practice in coordinating stakeholders and project teams from ambiguity to clarity, I have built strong cross-team collaboration skills and abilities to tackle complex design problems.
