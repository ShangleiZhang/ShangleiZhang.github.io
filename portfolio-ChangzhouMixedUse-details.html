Project Info

This is a mega-sized mixed-use project located in Changzhou, which includes 4 plots with a total GFA of 700,790 SQM, comprising a 4-storey shopping mall, 1 iconic hotel/office tower, 3 SOHO towers and a separated residential zone.

The project aims to create a vibrant district hub, which is not only severing the residential communities nearby, but also enhance a positive, meaningful relationship between the neighborhood and other parts of the city. A river passing through the project site generates the biggest challenge of connecting the mall space in different plots. In order to create a seamless one-mall space, a linking mall and connected roof top parking space over the water body are introduced in the scheme, to bridge customersâ€™ shopping experience from the both sides.
