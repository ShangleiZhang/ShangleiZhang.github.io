Guangzhou Dayi Villa

  • February11,2014
  • Architecture, Villa

Project Info

The villa is located on the plot S-60 of Dayi Villa Development in Guangzhou. The plot is surrounded by the access road in the North, a natural forest in the South, and neighboring villas in the East & West.

Two trumpet shell shaped volumes compose the living spaces, linked by an internal green corridor crossing the whole project from East to West. Bridges across this space will link the Northern and Southern parts of the house.

Vertical circulations are located at the eastern edge of the house. This core leads to every functions of the house, which are organized with gradually more privacy toward the West. The bedrooms are located at the western end of the house, enjoying complete privacy.
