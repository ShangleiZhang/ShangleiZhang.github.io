Infinity Canvas

  • Dec,2015
  • UX/UI Design

Project Info

"Infinity Canvas" is an online collaborative whiteboard specially designed for remote teamwork. We noticed that there were a bunch of similar products in the market. Based on our preliminary research, we* proposed several new feature functions to fill the gap of existing products.

Our ultimate goal is to bring "design thinking and creative collaboration" to a wider audience, facilitate creative, social collaboration among group members. We would like to create an environment which enables collaborative production of design and encourages “design thinking” for people engaging in collaborative design.

Currently, myself is still working on the online collaborative workspace with PRYSM, an Indianapolis based, professional IT solutions and services company.

*Team Member: Shanglei Zhang, Michele Walker, Susan Hirsch

Background

The Challenge

Online collaborative workspace came out because of the frustration of multi-location teamwork. The traditional diagramming tools would often be restrictive, with syntactic limitations and space limitations. We want to propose an intuitive, centralized shared workspace, which allows the whole team access the virtual whiteboard and co-work across distances.

The Opportunity

Many new online collaborative whiteboard products are springing up like mushrooms. We picked six of them to do walk-through study.

We want to compare those apps to each other in different criteria. We took into account the following criteria for the comparison: Freehand Precision, Tool Varieties, Text Input, Image Insert, External File Upload, Whiteboard Size, Group Chat, Share, Comments and lastly, Interface Simplicity.
